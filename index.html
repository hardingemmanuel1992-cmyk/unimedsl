UNIMED MENTAL HEALTH RESOURCE (INC)

RESTORING BROKEN LIVES

UNIMED Mental Health Resource (Inc) was founded on the backdrop of a true story that mental health is a disease, that it is real and does not discriminate. The organization was named after a young man whose dreams of becoming a mechanical engineer was shattered by mental illness. The organization later became UNIMED in 2023 after the passing of her second son. We treat individuals with mental illnesses through medication, therapy and skills training.

What Drives Us

Our Philosophy

Through education and the creation of the right environment, individuals can experience positive behavioral change. Our residential treatment center, BETHEL, provides a safe space where persons with substance abuse and mental health conditions are treated, rehabilitated, and empowered with skills to return to their communities as valued citizens.

Our Vision

Together with our Board, we provide comprehensive, coordinated programs supporting diagnosis, treatment, rehabilitation, and wellbeing of individuals in the United States, Sierra Leone and beyond. We create a world where mental illness is understood and every person has access to quality care.
