Our Admissions Process

Taking the first step toward recovery starts with our simple and supportive admissions process.

1

Initial Contact

Call us at +1 (817) 690-5032 or email us at info@unimedmh.org to discuss your needs and begin the process.

2

Assessment

Our intake team will conduct a comprehensive assessment to understand your situation, medical history, and treatment needs. This helps us determine the best approach for your recovery.

3

Treatment Planning

We'll develop a personalized treatment plan tailored to your specific needs and goals. Your plan may include medication management, therapy, skills training, and support group participation.

4

Begin Your Journey

Start your treatment with our compassionate and professional team committed to your recovery. We'll support you every step of the way toward lasting wellness and hope.

Ready to Take the First Step?

Contact us today to begin your journey to recovery. Our compassionate team is here to help.
