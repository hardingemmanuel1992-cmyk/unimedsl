BETHEL Residential Treatment Center

BETHEL's state-of-the-art facilities are designed to provide a safe, therapeutic environment for recovery. Our comfortable accommodations and well-maintained amenities support healing and growth.

Our Facilities

BETHEL features modern residential accommodations, therapy spaces, recreational areas, and medical facilities. Each environment is thoughtfully designed to promote comfort, dignity, and successful recovery. We maintain clean, safe standards across all areas.

Therapeutic Environment

Beyond physical structures, BETHEL creates a community of healing where residents support one another. Our facility fosters connection, hope, and the belief that recovery is possible. The environment itself becomes part of the treatment process.
