Our Mission Story

UNIMED Mental Health Resource (Inc) was founded on the backdrop of a true story that mental health is a disease, that it is real and does not discriminate. The organization was named after a young man whose dreams of becoming a mechanical engineer was shattered by mental illness.

BETHEL's Impact

Our residential treatment center, BETHEL, provides a safe space where persons with substance abuse and mental health conditions are treated, rehabilitated, and empowered with skills to return to their communities as valued citizens.

Global Initiative

Mrs. Doris Festus Lahai visited Kissy Mental Home in Sierra Leone and witnessed the dire conditions. UNIMED is committed to improving mental health care and support globally.

What We Do

UNIMED is a 501(c)(3) organization dedicated to restoring broken lives through medical treatment, psychological therapy, skills training and accommodation. We treat individuals with mental illnesses through evidence-based approaches and compassionate care.
