Invite to Rachel Program

The Invite to Rachel program is a community outreach and support initiative designed to help individuals and families affected by mental health challenges.

Program Overview

Invite to Rachel connects individuals struggling with mental health challenges to resources, support groups, and treatment options. This program recognizes that recovery often begins with a meaningful invitation to engage with community and professional support.

How It Works

Whether you're concerned about yourself or a loved one, Invite to Rachel provides information, guidance, and connections to mental health services. Our team helps you navigate the path to recovery and wellness with compassion and understanding.
