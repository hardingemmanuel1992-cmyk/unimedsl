Our dedicated team at BETHEL residential treatment center

BETHEL Residential Treatment Center Team

The BETHEL staff team is composed of experienced professionals committed to providing safe, therapeutic, and transformative care.

Our Team Philosophy

Our staff works collaboratively to support recovery and rehabilitation for individuals with substance abuse and mental health conditions. Through education and the creation of the right environment, individuals can experience positive behavioral change and return to their communities as valued citizens.

Dedicated Professionals

BETHEL staff includes psychiatrists, therapists, nurses, counselors, and support personnel trained in evidence-based treatment approaches. Each team member is committed to treating every individual with dignity, compassion, and respect.
