The Lahai's

Dedicated leaders and supporters of UNIMED's mission

The Lahai Family Leadership

Founding Vision

The Lahai family's deep commitment to mental health care stems from personal experience and a passion for helping others. Their dedication to UNIMED's mission reflects their belief that mental illness is a disease that deserves compassionate, professional treatment and community support.

Global Impact

Mrs. Doris Festus Lahai's visit to Kissy Mental Home in Sierra Leone revealed the critical mental health crisis affecting vulnerable populations. The Lahai family is committed to extending UNIMED's mission globally, bringing dignity and quality care to those most in need.

Our Commitment

The Lahai family's leadership has shaped UNIMED into an organization that treats individuals with mental illnesses through medication, therapy, and skills training. Their vision extends to Sierra Leone and beyond, where they work to improve mental health care and support vulnerable populations.

Leadership

Mrs. Doris Festus Lahai

Founder & CFO

Registered Nurse and Nurse Practitioner with extensive healthcare and organizational leadership background spanning multiple continents. Her passion for mental health advocacy stems from personal experience and her commitment to transforming lives.
